About Us

We are a group of UG1 students who understand the challenges of transitioning into life at FLAME. Our goal is to make this process easier for future UG1s by providing the guidance and resources we wish we had when we joined. Whether it’s navigating academics, campus life, or settling in, we’re here to help make your entry into FLAME as smooth as possible! Through our Digital Literacy course, we learned to code and built this website using HTML to share valuable insights and help new students have a smoother entry into FLAME.
