FLAME Kurukshetra

FLAME Kurukshetra is the flagship inter-collegiate festival organized by FLAME University in Pune, India. This annual event serves as a platform for students to showcase their talents across various domains, including sports, cultural activities, and management competitions. The festival aims to highlight the best in every aspect of competition, fostering a spirit of enthusiasm and excellence among participants.

FLAME Fest

FLAME Fest is the university's cultural festival, featuring competitions in gaming, poetry writing, creative writing, public speaking, advertising contests, dumb charades, treasure hunts, art marathons, singing, photography, dance, drama, and fashion shows. Students from different houses compete, with the house winning the most contests awarded the FLAME Fest trophy. The 2025 edition is currently underway, with participant registration forms recently released.
